Our Company History

JSU is a leading water purification company in Bursa with 15+ years of experience delivering high-quality water treatment solutions to families and businesses across the region. Since our inception, we have been committed to revolutionizing the way people access clean, safe, and healthy drinking water.

Over the past 15 years, JSU has grown from a small local water treatment business to become the most recognized and trusted water purification provider in Bursa. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, investment in cutting-edge technology, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We've served thousands of satisfied customers and established ourselves as industry leaders in water purification solutions.

Our Foundation: JSU was founded on the belief that access to clean drinking water is a fundamental right. We started with a simple mission to provide affordable, reliable water purification solutions to the Bursa community and have since expanded our reach to surrounding regions.

Our Mission

Mission Statement

To provide clean and safe drinking water solutions to families and businesses through innovative water purification technologies, exceptional customer service, and sustainable practices that protect our environment for future generations.

Our mission drives every decision we make at JSU. We are dedicated to:

  • Delivering superior water purification systems that meet international quality standards
  • Providing affordable pricing without compromising on quality or service
  • Offering comprehensive support and maintenance services to all our customers
  • Continuously improving our products and services based on customer feedback
  • Promoting awareness about water quality and the importance of water purification
