Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between RO and other filtration systems?

Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems use a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved solids and contaminants from water. RO systems achieve the highest purification rates (98-99%) compared to other methods. Our Express Water and Atlas products utilize advanced RO technology for superior water quality.

Which product should I choose for my home?

For residential use, we recommend Express Water for premium purification or Atlas for versatile, cost-effective solutions. Both offer easy installation, low maintenance, and excellent water quality. Aqualine is ideal if you prioritize environmental sustainability.

How often do filters need replacement?

Filter replacement schedules vary based on water quality and usage. Typically, pre-filters last 6-12 months, while RO membranes last 2-3 years. We provide maintenance schedules and professional support for all our products.

Are these systems suitable for commercial use?

Yes! Ravent is specifically designed for industrial applications with high capacity. Atlas also supports commercial installations. Both offer scalable solutions and professional technical support.

What makes these products certified and trusted?

All our products meet international water quality standards and certifications. Express Water is the most trusted RO system in Turkey with proven reliability. We conduct rigorous testing to ensure 99% purification efficiency and safety standards compliance.

Do you offer installation and support services?

Yes! We provide professional installation services, maintenance support, and 24/7 customer service. Our experienced technicians can handle both residential and industrial installations with minimal downtime.
